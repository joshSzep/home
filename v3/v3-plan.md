## Core Vision

Rewrite from scratch to create a richer, more immersive meditation on mortality and meaning. Maintain the philosophical depth and emotional honesty while:

1. **Deepening world-building** — Make 3025 Earth feel lived-in and real
2. **Expanding middle sections** — Give Years 5-10 proper breathing room
3. **Balancing POV distribution** — Ensure all 7 characters get equal development
4. **Adding texture** — More daily life, more sensory detail, more humanity
5. **Sharpening thematic resonance** — Make the Five Prisons and Forever Earth doctrine central

**Seven answers** (one per character):

- **David**: Process matters (maintenance as ritual)
- **Zara**: Creation matters (life despite doom)
