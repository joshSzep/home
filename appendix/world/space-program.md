## Space Program After the Asteroid (3025-3040)

### Year 1 (3025-3026): The False Hope

**Can We Deflect It?**
- Immediate global response: try to stop it
- Every space agency mobilized
- Plans for: nuclear deflection, kinetic impact, gravity tractor
- Unified human effort (briefly)

**Why It Fails**
- **Scale: asteroid too massive**
- **Velocity: too fast, approaching too directly**
- **No fusion: can't generate enough energy**
- **Physics: we're earthbound for reasons - this is one**
- Timeline too short: 15 years not enough
- Even unified humanity: can't do it
- Would need technology we don't have
- Would need time we don't have
- **Would need to not be trapped by physics**

**Space Infrastructure Mobilized**
- Orbital facilities: all hands on deflection efforts
- Moon bases: preparing launch missions
- Mars: evacuated (why maintain it now?)
- Asteroid mining: repurposed
- Every resource thrown at problem
- Years 1-3: desperately trying

**The Abandonment (Years 3-5)**
- Calculation after calculation: can't be done
- Attempts fail
- Hope dies
- Space program: what's the point now?
- Mars: evacuated completely (confirmed)
- Moon: bases abandoned
- Orbital stations: crews return to Earth
- Why be in space when Earth is ending?
- **Want to be home for the end**
