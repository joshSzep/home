## History & Construction

### The Building of Antarctica

**Timeline:**
- 2100s: First scientific bases expanded
- 2200s: Ice begins melting, first permanent settlements
- 2300-2500: Mass migration from drowned coasts and hot zones
- 2400-2800: Peak construction period
- 2800-3025: Consolidation, infilling, continuous growth

### Why Antarctica?
- **Raw necessity: few places on Earth suitable for human habitation**
- Temperate climate after warming
- No native population to displace
- Neutral territory (collectively owned)
- Vast available land
- UN-governed from the start

### How Was It Built?
- **Powered by solar arrays built in parallel**
- Massive international effort
- Corporate-funded construction (energy corps especially)
- Robotic labor as technology advanced
- Generation after generation adding to sprawl
- No master plan - organic, chaotic growth
- Different cultures built different districts
