## Geography (Pre-Asteroid)

### Sea Levels
- **No permanent ice reserves on Earth**
- All ice melted over centuries (gradual, not sudden event)
- **Sea level rise: ~70 meters (+230 feet)**
- This is full melt of Greenland (~7m), Antarctica (~58m), glaciers (~1m), thermal expansion (~4m+)
- Current sea level vs. 2025: catastrophically higher
- Coastal changes: entire coastlines redrawn
- **This happened gradually over 200-400 years**

### Flooded Cities
- **All low-lying coastal cities: underwater and abandoned**

- Ruins underwater - diving sites, archaeological zones
- Cultural grief over lost cities
